Executive Summary

Customer credits is a validated, high-priority gap with consistent demand spanning many years. 897 Reforge snippets and multiple Facebook discussion threads document ongoing requests, while internal Jira tickets (XB-222, TSI-19) and Support-documented workarounds confirm this is a well-known pain point. The current state is that pros have no clean way to record and apply customer credits‚Äîthey're forced into painful manual workarounds like tracking credits in customer notes (which get lost), creating fake unscheduled jobs (which clutter the system), or managing dual systems with QuickBooks (which don't sync). Support literally documents "no clean way" to do this. The result is what customers describe as "bookkeeping chaos": techs miss credits leading to angry customers, accounting reconciliation breaks down for large companies, and in at least one documented case, the gap creates churn risk.

The scope of this problem is significant. Pros need credits for overpayments (most common use case), service mistakes and compensation, referral programs, prepayments and deposits, and bulk B2B payments. Every workaround is either manual and error-prone, or requires external systems that don't integrate. The business impact extends beyond operational pain: pros cannot formalize referral programs (blocking customer acquisition strategy), QuickBooks integration creates credit memos that HCP can't manage (revenue/tax timing issues), and customers describe administrative burden that affects their willingness to stay on the platform. Competitively, this is table stakes‚Äîboth ServiceTitan and Jobber have credits features, and customers transitioning from ServiceTitan explicitly expect this capability. This is not a new problem or a newly discovered gap; it's a long-standing, well-documented need that affects core workflows and competitive positioning.

Agent 3: Payment Processors
‚ñº

Key Findings

  • Stripe: ONLY processor with native, robust Customer Balance features (automatic application, full API)
  • Square: NO native credits (requested since 2016, never built - workarounds all have major drawbacks)
  • PayPal: NO merchant-managed credits (only customer wallet)

Bottom Line

Stripe is the only viable choice if credits are critical. Use Stripe Customer Balance as storage layer, manage business logic in HCP.

Agent 4: Consumer Platforms
‚ñº

Key Findings
